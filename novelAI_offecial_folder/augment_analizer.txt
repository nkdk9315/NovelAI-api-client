payload

promptに入る単語は
{neutral;;, happy;;, sad;;, angry;;, scared;;, surprised;;, tired;;, excited;;, nervous;;, thinking;;, confused;;, shy;;, disgusted;;, smug;;, bored;;, laughing;;, irritated;;, aroused;;, embarrassed;;, love;;, worried;;, determined;;, hurt;;, playful;;}

defryは0~5　（5が最弱、0が最強）
